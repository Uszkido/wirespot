## What To Include

For faster support, include:

- WireSpot app version.
- Android phone model and Android version.
- RouterOS version and board model.
- Whether the router is local LAN or WireGuard remote.
- The screen or feature affected.
- Steps to reproduce the issue.
- Screenshots with secrets removed.
- Logs with router passwords, WireGuard keys, and customer data removed.

## Support Areas

Supported:

- App setup and first launch.
- Router add/test connection.
- RouterOS API troubleshooting.
- WireGuard import and connection guidance.
- Hotspot users, sessions, profiles, and vouchers.
- Bluetooth thermal printer setup.
- Reports, backup/restore, settings, licensing, and co-branding.
- Play Store/release preparation for maintainers.

Not supported through public issues:

- Real router passwords or private WireGuard configs.
- Production customer data.
- Android signing keys or Play Billing secrets.
- Security vulnerabilities. Use [SECURITY.md](SECURITY.md).

## Response Expectations

WireSpot is in active development and field testing. Support is handled on a
best-effort basis, with priority for:

1. Security issues.
2. Router connection failures.
3. WireGuard/VPN failures.
4. Voucher, payment, or licensing issues.
5. Printer and report polish.
